ABOUT US

Nestled in the heart of Cebu, Petrosial Hotel is your gateway to luxury and convenience. Offering stylish accommodations and breathtaking city views, our hotel is perfectly situated for both business and leisure travelers. With easy access to key destinations, including transport hubs and commercial centers, we ensure a seamless stay for our guests.

Our hotel features well-appointed rooms with modern amenities, state-of-the-art meeting spaces, and a grand ballroom designed for memorable gatherings. Take a refreshing dip in our serene pool, maintain your fitness routine at our fully equipped gym, or indulge in an exquisite dining experience at our signature restaurant, serving a delightful array of international and local cuisine.

Built on a foundation of excellence, Petrosial Hotel stands as a landmark of comfort and sophistication, reflecting Cebu’s dynamic growth while preserving its rich heritage. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, we invite you to experience a stay like no other.
